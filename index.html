What you are looking at

This website is not collecting your real data. Instead, it stages an interface inspired by the dashboards that silently watch us in the background – estimating how long we scroll, when we can’t sleep, and what we will buy next.

In the broader Algorithmic Cage storyworld, this console is one of many ways to tell the same story: a user reduced to a file name, a cluster of tags, and a set of probabilities. The written report shows the cold language of an internal document. The video zooms out to reveal millions of glowing points on a world map. This site invites you to press the button yourself and feel what it is like to be “read” by a model.
